About Drive Fix

Drive Fix is a Vehicle Service and Ride Management System developed to provide a convenient platform for booking rides and scheduling vehicle services. It combines transportation and vehicle maintenance into one easy-to-use web application.

The system helps users book rides, schedule vehicle servicing, track service progress, and make online payments. Drivers and service providers efficiently manage their assigned tasks, while the admin oversees all platform activities.

Our Vision

To become a trusted digital platform that simplifies transportation and vehicle maintenance by providing reliable, fast, and secure services for everyone.

Our Mission

Our mission is to improve customer convenience by integrating ride booking and vehicle servicing into one system, ensuring better communication, transparency, and efficiency.

Key Features

  • Online Ride Booking
  • Vehicle Service Scheduling
  • Real-Time Ride Tracking
  • Real-Time Service Status Tracking
  • Online Payment Facility
  • User Profile Management
  • Driver Management
  • Service Provider Management
  • Admin Dashboard
  • Reports and Analytics

System Modules

  1. Admin User Management
    Admin manages customers, drivers, and service providers.
  2. Booking & Ride Oversight
    Admin monitors all ride and service bookings.
  3. Reports & Analytics
    Generates reports and monitors overall platform performance.
  4. Service Provider Module
    Service providers update service status and maintain service records.
  5. Driver Ride Management
    Drivers accept ride requests and update ride status.
  6. User Booking Module
    Users book rides and schedule vehicle services.
  7. Status Tracking
    Users monitor ride and vehicle service progress.
  8. Profile & Feedback
    Users manage profiles and submit valuable feedback.

Benefits of Drive Fix

  • Easy and secure ride booking.
  • Quick vehicle service scheduling.
  • Reduced waiting time.
  • Improved communication between users and service providers.
  • Transparent service tracking.
  • Convenient online payments.
  • Efficient management through a centralized platform.
